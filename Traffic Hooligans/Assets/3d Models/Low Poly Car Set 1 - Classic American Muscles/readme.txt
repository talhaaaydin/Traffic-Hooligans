1. INTRO
Thank you for purchasing the Low Poly Car Set 1 - Classic American Muscles!
You are free to use the models in your own project in any way you like. All models are low-poly and mobile ready!


2. CONTENT
This package contains 3 classical car models:

Musclecar1 - 1047 verts, 1201 faces, 1453 tris
Musclecar2 - 1607 verts, 1271 faces, 1615 tris 
Musclecar3 - 1324 verts, 1148 faces, 1387 tris

Each model comes with two different paintjobs. Textures are in .PNG format and easy to change.
